My favorite city : Zitacuaro

Zitácuaro is a beautiful and historic city located in the state of Michoacán. It is world-renowned for being one of the key areas for the hibernation of the majestic monarch butterfly, offering a truly breathtaking natural spectacle in its nearby sanctuaries.

Besides its natural beauty, Zitácuaro stands out for its great importance in Mexican history, its delicious local cuisine, and the warmth of its people, making it a unique place steeped in tradition.
